Welcome to Viva Beach Hotel

The hotel opened its door for our guests in 2006, and since then, we daily provide comfort and jolly atmosphere to everyone, who has crossed our threshold. You will find us easily – just follow your heart. The hotel is located 50 m. away from the south beach in Pomorie, in the immediate vicinity of the five stars hotel complex “Sunset resort” and “Festa Pomorie Resort”. Pomorie town is located at a distance of 18 km northeast of Bourgas, at 8 km of the Bourgas airport, at 20 km of the “Sunny Beach” Sea resort, and at 408 km east of our capital city Sofia.

In an immediate vicinity to the hotel, there is a bus station to the old part of Pomorie, to Bourgas, and Sunny Beach. The distance from the hotel to the centre of the town is about 3 km.

VIVA BEACH offers its guests: two double-bed rooms, two triple rooms apartment for 4 people, apartment for 4+2 people, and apartment for 6 people. Each room has its own bathroom and WC, balcony, air-conditioner, minibar and cable TV. On the ground floor there are the reception, the sauna, the fitness room, and the bistro where you could have delicious food form our national or the European cuisine. The hotel is operating through the whole year.
